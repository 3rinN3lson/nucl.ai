Which Ticket is the ONE for You?

Conference

If you want to get a broad selection of sessions about what's new in AI, which thigs have been implemented in recent games, then the Conference ticket is the best choice for you. It also gives you many opportunities to catch up with fellow AI enthusiasts and get inspired by the many new fields that currently influence AI.

All Access

Go All Access if you want to dive into the topics deeper with special masterclasses and tutorials. Also, this ticket gives you networking opportunities with experts in the field and time to exchange with the speakers . Never worry to miss a session - you can always catch up with it later by watching the recordings!
