Which Ticket is Best for You?

Conference

If you want to get a broad selection of sessions about what's new and innovative in AI, inspiring and entertaining keynotes, or techniques/algorithms implemented into recent games, then the Conference ticket is the best choice for you.

It also gives you many opportunities to catch up with fellow AI enthusiasts and get inspired by the many new fields that currently influence AI.

All Access

Go All Access if you want to dive into the topics deeper with specialized masterclasses, in-depth tutorials, and advanced panel discussions. Also, this ticket gives you additional networking opportunities (e.g. over lunch) with experts in the field and time to exchange with the speakers.

Also, you never need to worry about missing a session; you can always catch up with it later by watching the recordings online!

Frequently Asked Questions

Ticket Upgrade

If you'd like to upgrade the ticket you have purchased please go to EventBrite.com and update your order information. How to upgrade your ticket in Eventbrite. You can also find the link to your order in the section "Your Account" of your confirmation email.
You can upgrade your ticket until July 13th. After this date we unfortunately cannot guarantee a spot at the networking lunches or the V.I.P. dinner.

Ticket Transfer

Tickets can be transferred to an other person free of charge until July 6th. To do so please update your order information with the new attendee's information on Eventbrite.com. You can also find the link to your order in the section "Your Account" of your confirmation email. If you have received a special discount such as Student, Member, or Indie you must contact us before transferring as the new attendee needs to be eligible for this discount as well. How to transfer your ticket in Eventbrite.

Ticket Refund

If you need a ticket refund please contact us at team(at)aigamedev.com. Conference tickets can be refunded until June 20th minus a service charge of € 15. All Access tickets can be refunded until July 6th minus a service charge of € 15. After these dates or in case of a no-show of the attendee ticket price refunds are not possible.

Help?

If you need any assistance, we're happy to help! You can contact us at events (at) aigamedev.com
