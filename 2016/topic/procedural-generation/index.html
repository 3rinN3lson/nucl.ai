Procedural Content & Generative Systems

The nucl.ai Conference is the largest worldwide event focused on applications of Artificial Intelligence in Creative Industries. Join us in Vienna this July for three action-packed days!

Now in its 8th edition, the nucl.ai Conference 2016 on July 18-20 will bring together hundreds of programmers, artists, hackers, designers, creative coders working on AI—ranging from robotics to games, visual effects to architecture, mobile apps to online bots, simulations or exhibits. It's a great place for developers to exchange ideas and find inspiration!

Proceduralism is now becoming more common place as algorithms take over the low-level tasks of content-creation pipelines, but also generative systems at the high-level that create entire worlds or experiences at runtime. Find out how to integrate generative algorithms into your pipelines and design processes.
